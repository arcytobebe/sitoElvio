Hi, I’m Elvio! I’m an Italian photographer, currently based between Milan and Barcelona.
Through my work, I aim to create images capable to form a visual reality expressive communicatively and aesthetically. People are always at the heart of my visual exploration: the human figure is what drives every project I undertake.
Born as self-taught creative, before fully committing to photography, I earned a degree in Communication and Contemporary Media.
I primarily work in the fields of fashion, advertising, and music, using color, imagery, and multimedia influences to convey my artistic vision and personal identity, which I feel “pop”.
